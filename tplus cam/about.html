About Toyota Plus Cambodia

Cambodia's most trusted certified pre-owned Toyota dealership since 2016

8+ Years of Trust
Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Quality

Toyota Plus Cambodia was founded in 2016 with a simple mission: to give Cambodian customers access to high-quality, fairly priced pre-owned Toyota vehicles they can trust.

We are an authorized certified pre-owned Toyota dealer based in Phnom Penh, serving customers across Cambodia including Siem Reap, Battambang, Sihanoukville and beyond.

Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a rigorous 150-point inspection by our factory-trained Toyota technicians, so you can drive away with complete confidence.

Our Journey

Our Story, Year by Year

From a single showroom to Cambodia's most trusted Toyota dealer

2016

Founded in Phnom Penh

Toyota Plus Cambodia opens its doors on Monivong Boulevard with a team of 8 and a vision to bring trust to the used car industry.

2017

First 100 Cars Sold

We reached our first major milestone -- 100 happy customers. Word of mouth began driving growth across Phnom Penh.

2018

Workshop Expansion

Opened our dedicated inspection and service workshop, staffed by Toyota-trained technicians, to ensure every car meets our exacting standards.

2019

Toyota CPO Authorized

Received official Toyota Certified Pre-Owned authorization, becoming one of Cambodia's few officially recognized CPO dealers.

2020

Online Showroom Launch

Launched our digital inventory platform, allowing customers across Cambodia to browse, filter, and enquire about vehicles from anywhere.

2022

Bank Partnerships

Established official financing partnerships with ABA Bank and ACLEDA Bank, making car ownership accessible to more Cambodian families.

2023

1,000 Vehicles Sold

A landmark moment -- our 1,000th vehicle handover, with customers now spanning every province across Cambodia.

2025

5,000+ Happy Customers

Today, Toyota Plus Cambodia is trusted by over 5,000 customers. We continue to grow, improve, and earn the trust of every Cambodian family we serve.
